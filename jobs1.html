Job Overview

The company is a small-scale IT education organization specializing in online courses and training programs for tech skills. We offers interactive learning modules, certification programs, and hands-on projects to help students gain practical experience in fields such as software development, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and data science.

As a Database Administrator (DBA) in the DevOps Team, you will be responsible for managing, maintaining, and optimizing the company’s databases to ensure high availability, security, and efficiency. You will collaborate closely with developers and system administrators to implement database automation, monitor performance, and support continuous integration and deployment (CI/CD) processes. Your role is crucial in ensuring data integrity and enabling scalable solutions for the company’s online learning platform.

    Responsibilities

  • Administer and maintain relational (MySQL, PostgreSQL) and NoSQL (MongoDB, DynamoDB) databases.
  • Monitor database performance and troubleshoot slow queries, bottlenecks, and downtime issues.
  • Optimize database queries, indexing, and schema design for efficiency.
  • Ensure database security by managing user permissions, access controls, and encryption.
  • Automate database maintenance tasks using scripts and monitoring tools.
  • Collaborate with developers and DevOps engineers to support data-driven applications.
  • Stay up to date with the latest database technologies and best practices.

    Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or a related field.
  • 5+ years of experience as a Database Administrator in a production environment, preferably education-related.
  • Strong grasp of SQL, database performance tuning, and query optimization.
  • Experience working with cloud-based databases (AWS RDS, Google Cloud SQL, Azure SQL, etc.) and database management software, such as Oracle, SQL Server, or MySQL.
  • Familiarity with database design, backup strategies, replication, and high-availability architectures.
  • Understanding of database security, compliance, and access control.
  • Experience with scripting languages, with mastery of at least once (Python, Bash, or PowerShell) for automation.
