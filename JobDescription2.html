Job Overview

Company is a dedicated online learning platform that provides access to world-class courses, certificates, and degrees from top universities and organizations worldwide. The company focuses on expanding education accessibility through technology-driven learning experiences.

As a As an IT Support at Company, you will be responsible for providing technical support to employees, ensuring smooth IT operations, troubleshooting hardware/software issues, and maintaining IT infrastructure. You will work closely with other teamss to improve efficiency and enhance the user experience for internal systems.

    Responsibilities

  • Provide first-line technical support for employees, resolving hardware, software, and network issues.
  • Timely respond to IT support requests via request ticketing system, email, or in-person interactions.
  • Install, configure, and maintain company devices (laptops, desktops, printers, and mobile devices).
  • Manage user accounts, permissions, and security settings in systems like Google Workspace and Active Directory.
  • Troubleshoot network connectivity issues and assist with VPN and remote access setup.
  • Maintain IT asset inventory and ensure proper equipment allocation.
  • Assist with software updates, security patches, and system upgrades.
  • Collaborate with IT teams to implement new tools and improve workflow efficiency.

    Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or a related field.
  • 2+ years of experience in IT support, helpdesk, or technical support roles.
  • Strong knowledge of Windows, macOS, and Linux operating systems.
  • Experience troubleshooting networking, VPN, and cloud-based applications (Google Workspace, Office 365, etc.)
  • Familiarity with Active Directory, IT asset management, and endpoint security tools.
